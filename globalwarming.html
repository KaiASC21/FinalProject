Climate Change

Let's start with defining the two words in climate change. Climate is the usual weather of a certain place. At times it is cold, but other times it is sunny and hot. The climate varies around the world. Earth's climate is the climate we get from combining all the climates in the world. Change means becoming something different, new, or unusual. Historically, Earth's climate has always been changing. The distance from the sun changes resulting in hotter and colder temperatures. However, this time it is caused by us humans. We burn coal, drive cars, emitting gases. The emittion of these gases are lethal changing the climate of Earth. It is predicted by scientists that Earth's temperature is on a rise for the next 100 years. As a result, icebergs will melt at a faster pace and ocean levels will rise. The effect of climate change is impacting millions of people. Rising sea levels causes the displacement of many people fromtheir homes, especially those living by the shore or on islands.
