Our mission is to act as a one-stop place for people to educate themselves about various environmental problems, and hopefully learn tips that they can use to implement in their everyday life. Furthermore, becoming a better citizen of this planet.


The Problem:

Every action we make has an impact on the environment around us. Taking public transportation, instead of a car can result in a major difference. Our summers are getting hotter and hotter each year. This July was the hottest month on record! Not only are we humans affected, but animals and plants are losing their habitat due to our negligence of the environment. As citizens of earth, it is our duty to keep our environment in shape.

Our solution:

We believe that the main reason our environment is falling apart is due to the lack of information. Therefore, becoming a problem that is snowballing out of control. By making an informative website such as this we can get thousands of people nationwide more informed on these issues, leading us to solutions. Although some may believe ignorance is bliss, in this case it is not. We have to take control of our environmental problem. Especially, the youth as we are the people who will deal with this situation in the coming years.
